Acme Corp Employee Handbook (Sample)

1. Remote Work Policy
Acme Corp employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work
requests must be approved by a direct manager at least one week in
advance. Employees working remotely are expected to be reachable on
Slack during core hours, 10am-4pm in their local timezone.

2. Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of PTO per year, prorated for new
hires based on start date. PTO accrues monthly and can be carried over
up to a maximum of 10 unused days into the following calendar year.
Unused PTO beyond the carryover cap is forfeited, not paid out.

3. Expense Reimbursement
Employees can expense up to $50/month for home internet and up to
$75/month for a co-working space, with a manager's prior approval.
Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase through the
finance portal. Reimbursements are processed within two pay cycles.

4. Parental Leave
Acme Corp offers 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for the primary
caregiver and 8 weeks for the secondary caregiver, available to all
full-time employees regardless of tenure. Leave can be taken in one
continuous block or split into two blocks within the first 12 months
after the qualifying event.

5. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews happen twice a year, in June and December. Each
review includes a self-assessment, manager feedback, and peer feedback
from at least two colleagues. Compensation changes resulting from
reviews take effect the following pay period.

6. Equipment Policy
New employees receive a laptop, monitor, and a one-time $300 home-office
setup stipend within their first 30 days. Laptops are refreshed every 3
years or sooner if hardware fails and is out of warranty.
